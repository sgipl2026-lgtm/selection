Scoring Rules & Game Rules

Scroll down on the home screen to find two collapsible panels. Click either heading to expand them and read the rules before you start playing.

📸 Scoring Rules (left) and Game Rules (right) — both expanded

📊 How points are scored — every action by a player in your XI earns or costs you points:

🏏 Batting
Run scored+1 pt
Four hit+6 pts
Six hit+10 pts
50-run milestone+25 pts
100-run milestone+50 pts
Duck (0 runs)−10 pts
Strike Rate >150 (min 10 balls)+10 pts

Wicket taken+10 pts

4+ wicket haul+50 pts

Catch taken+25 pts

⏰ Key timing rules you must know:
• Email reminders are sent 4 hours and 2 hours before each match.
• Selections are locked 30 minutes before match start — no changes after that.
• If you miss the deadline, your score for that match is 0 points.
• Results and the updated leaderboard are emailed the day after each match.
3
Log In & Choose a Match

Once signed in successfully, you'll see all upcoming IPL matches on the selection screen.

📸 After login — all available matches shown with date and time
  • 🟢 Matches marked OPEN are available for selection. Tap any match card to start picking your team for that game.
  • 👤 Your name appears at the top of the screen confirming you're logged in. The 📊 My Dashboard button also appears here.
  • 📅 Each match card shows the two teams, the date, and the kick-off time (IST). Plan ahead — don't wait until the last minute!
  • 🔄 You can submit a team for multiple matches. After submitting one, tap Submit for Another Match to pick for the next game.
💡 You can update your selection for a match as many times as you like — right up to the 30-minute lock window before that match starts.
4
Pick Your 11 Players

Tap any open match to enter the player selection screen. Both teams' full squads are shown side by side with roles and filters.

📸 Player picker — both team squads with roles, search bar, and position filters
🎯 Pick exactly 11 players

You must select exactly 11 players in total from the two playing teams combined.

⚖️ Min 4 from each team

At least 4 players from each team — maximum 7 from one side. You cannot pick all 11 from one team.

  • 🔍Search: Use the search box to quickly find a player by name.
  • 🏷️Filter by role: Tap All / Batsman / Bowler / AR / WK to narrow the list and build a balanced team.
  • ✅Tap to select: Tap a player's name to add them — a numbered badge (1–11) appears. Tap again or tap the chip at the bottom to remove.
  • 📊Live progress bar: Three checkboxes at the bottom track your progress — 11 players, Min 4 from each team, No duplicates. All three must be green to submit.
📸 11 players selected (6 KKR + 5 RCB), all validation checks green, winner prediction visible
🌟 Pro tip: All-Rounders (AR) are great value — they can earn points through both batting and bowling in the same match!
5
Predict the Winner & Submit

Once all three validation checks are green, scroll down to see the winner prediction and the submit button.

  • 🏆Predict the Winner: Two large buttons appear — one per team. Tap whichever team you think will win. Get it right for a bonus +50 pts!
  • ⚡Submit: Tap the big ⚡ SUBMIT MY FANTASY XI button. Your team is instantly saved and locked in.
📸 Submission successful — full XI summary, teams, and predicted winner shown
✅ Team Locked In! — The confirmation screen shows your complete Fantasy XI, your picks from each team, and your predicted winner.

Tap Submit for Another Match to go back and pick a team for the next fixture!
🔒 Selections are locked 30 minutes before match start. Always submit with time to spare!
6
View Rankings & Scores on the Dashboard

After each match is processed, click the Dashboard to see everyone's scores, ranks, and the overall leaderboard.

📸 The blue 📊 My Dashboard button appears in the header after login
  • 📊Find the button: After logging in, look for the 📊 My Dashboard button just below the game title in the header (highlighted in the screenshot above).
  • 🖥️Open it: Tap the button — a full-screen panel opens showing match scores, player points breakdown, and overall rankings for all participants.
  • 🔄Refresh: Tap ↺ Refresh in the top bar to check for the latest results.
  • ✕Close: Tap ✕ in the top-right corner to return to the main game screen.
📧 Also sent by Email

Results and the updated leaderboard are emailed to all players the day after each match — so you'll always know where you stand.

🕐 When is it updated?

The dashboard is generated automatically once match data is processed — usually within a day after the match ends.

⚡ Quick Recap — How to Play

  1. Open the game link and sign in with your Name and PIN.
  2. Pick a match from the upcoming fixtures list.
  3. Select 11 players — at least 4 from each team, max 7 from one.
  4. Predict the winner for a bonus 50 points.
  5. Submit before the 30-minute pre-match lock deadline.
  6. Check the Dashboard or your email the next day for results and rankings.
  7. Repeat for every match — the player with the most cumulative points at the end of IPL wins! 🏆
